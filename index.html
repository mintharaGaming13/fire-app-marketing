One unified overview — see your whole portfolio at a glance

Problem: Positions get out of sync after reinvestments and new contributions.

Solution: Fire Checkpoint keeps totals and position counts updated automatically.

  • Always accurate holdings — even after dividend reinvestments or new contributions
  • Dividend history summaries (12-month view)
  • One total view — across all accounts

Allocation clarity — know exactly what’s off target

Problem: It is hard to see when your allocation drifts from targets.

Solution: Fire Checkpoint highlights drift and shows exactly where you are off.

  • Clear targets — custom categories that match your plan
  • Drift warnings — see where you’re off at a glance
  • Rebalancing guidance — what to buy or trim

Smart cash flow — keep new money aligned

Problem: Bonuses and withdrawals often break your target mix.

Solution: Get a recommended split that keeps your allocation on target.

  • Aligned allocations — even after deposits or withdrawals
  • Simple guidance — ideal for bonuses and vesting events
  • Drawdown friendly — keep the target mix intact

Category insight — spot concentration risk early

Problem: Category-level risk hides inside spreadsheet tabs.

Solution: Slice your portfolio by asset class or any custom category.

  • Clear visuals — charts, bars, and lists
  • Risk visibility — catch concentration quickly
  • Trend tracking — drift over time

Honest progress — see how close you really are

Problem: Income projections can be misleading when dividends fluctuate.

Solution: See FIRE progress based on real dividend history and coverage.

  • Clear coverage — needs, wants, and total
  • Realistic income — based on dividend history, not just prices
  • Simple progress view — no fluff

Spending confidence — stay inside safe limits in volatile markets

Problem: Spending targets feel risky when markets move quickly.

Solution: Guardrails adjust your spending range based on portfolio performance.

  • Clear limits — set upper and lower ranges
  • Adaptive spending — aligned with coverage and runway
  • Explicit trade-offs — in real time

Downside protection — see your runway in months

Problem: Downside protection is hard to measure in a spreadsheet.

Solution: See cash and conservative runway in months at a glance.

  • Clear runway — cash runway in months
  • Conservative view — cash + bonds runway
  • More optionality — know your buffers
